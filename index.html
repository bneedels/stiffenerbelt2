Ultimate CCW Belt

Our best selling CCW Belt Reinforced with a Kydex Stiffener and backed with rugged and lightweight 20 year Nylon.

SCROLL DOWN FOR MORE INFO

All Hanks Belts are USA MADE of USA TANNED LEATHER...

Premium 13 OZ USA Tanned Leather

We didn't want to compromise the leatehr quaility on this belt. The result is the same extra heavy duty leather in our best selling Amish CCW belt...no compromises. Same thickness, same durabilty, in short...exactly the same leather.

Nylon Backing Over Kydex Stiffener

Why Nylon backing and not leather?
1. Cost
2. Thickness
3.Ease of On and Off

Quaility leather is expensive and thick. If we used 2 pieces of our 13oz leather the belt would be more money and to thick for use! If we split the leather down to manage cost and thickness we are giving up what makes a Hanks Belt so great in ther first place...the extra thick full grain one piece solid leather. By using a Nylon backing over leather we kept the same leather quaility with the added stiffining properties of the Kydex. In doing this we created an additional great feature...
Ease of putting the belt on and off. The Nylon backing is low friction which results in effortless removal and putting on of the belt. It slides right on and off with ease! This could come in handy if you quickly need to remove your belt and firearm holster for whatever reason.
